OPEN-PBL LMS

LMS for managing problem-based learning strategies


What it is? OPEN-PBL LMS is a Learning Management System (LMS) with a focus on Problem-Based Learning (PBL), which is developed as open source to the software developer community.

How it works? OPEN-PBL LMS manages the interaction between teachers, students and content for the correct implementation of the PBL learning strategy. OPEN-PBL LMS assists: 1. Teachers in the creation, dissemination, application and evaluation of students' performance in the "problem-case" discussion classes (CP). 2. Students in the research of academic topics prerequisites of problem cases and promotes interaction between members of groups of students trained to prepare the arguments for presentation in class.

How it works? Among the macro features of the OPEN-PBL LMS, the following stand out: 1. Assistance to teachers in the creation of case-problems (CP); 2. Creation of digital environments to assist in face-to-face classes for discussion of CP; 3. Management of the dissemination of the CP material and of all complementary material for its preliminary study before classes; 4. Management of the digital interaction environments of the groups formed for the preliminary study of CP; 5. Assessment of students' proficiency in subjects, CP pre-requisites; 6. Teacher assistance in the administration of the face-to-face session for discussion of the CP; 7. Registration in audiovisual archive of the participation of the students in the classes for discussion of the CP; 8. Evaluation and classification of students' participation in classes for discussion of CP;

Who shoud use? Through the use of OPEN-PBL LMS, schools and educational institutions have access to a complete solution for the management of student-centered and problem-solving education systems. Use of the base code available at open-pbl.org is free.
